# Tough Tongue AI — Research Summary

Research conducted to inform the design of Pre-Meeting Prep mode (Mode 3).

## What It Is

Tough Tongue AI is a multimodal conversation practice platform. Users rehearse difficult conversations (interviews, negotiations, sales calls) with adaptive AI agents. Founded by Ajitesh Abhishek.

## Core Features

1. **Agentic Voice AI** — Agents think, remember, and adapt in real-time. Sub-second response times.
2. **Multimodal Feedback** — Voice interaction + video analysis (facial expressions, posture) + post-session scoring.
3. **Four Practice Modes:**
   - Freedom Mode (open-ended)
   - Scene Mode (pre-built scenarios)
   - Custom Scenes (user-created, saveable)
   - Video Analysis (non-verbal feedback)
4. **Post-Session Debrief** — Structured feedback: verbal communication, body language, technical accuracy, actionable suggestions.

## What We're Taking for Presence

### Replicating (lightweight)
- **Custom scenario from context** — user describes meeting, gets tailored practice partner
- **Voice-first interaction** — sub-second latency critical for natural practice
- **Structured post-session scoring** — concrete metrics, not just "good job"
- **Zero-friction start** — "click and talk" simplicity for 5-min-before-meeting use case
- **Session memory** — track improvement over time

### Skipping (too heavy for menubar app)
- Whiteboards, code editors, slides integration
- Video analysis (adds friction)
- Extended practice sessions (we're doing 3-5 min drills)
- Full web platform features

### Doing Differently
- **Calendar integration** to auto-surface and pre-fill context (Tough Tongue doesn't do this)
- **Auto-transition to Meeting Coach** after rehearsal (unique to Presence)
- **Audio-only** (skip video to reduce friction)
- **Meeting-specific briefing** from calendar data rather than generic scenarios
